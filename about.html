Hello there, my name is Alex Lew.

I am an urban planner and transportation planner with experience in public transit, multi-modal transportation, and airport planning. My work stems from a life-long passion for transportation options.

I approach transportation planning with a multi-disciplinary toolbox that includes data visualization and analysis, geographic information systems (GIS), graphic design, as well as coding/programming. This site will soon be the home to various mapping, data visualization, and other sample projects from my portfolio.

I am graduate of Harvard's Graduate School of Design and Columbia University.
